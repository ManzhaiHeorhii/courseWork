Join the Army

Reasons why you should do it:

  • You are patriotic and believe in serving your country and people
  • You want a challenging job that will build you up physically, emotionally, educationally in job and technical skills, including overcoming your own fears and weaknesses
  • You want an opportunity to see and work in different places around the world, meet many different peoples including those of other nations
  • You don’t want to be tied down in a desk job or factory, but want a dynamic work environment that varies during the day, and from day to day, including outdoors, day and night, and different climates
  • You want a job that has a lot of opportunity for advancement and promotion
  • You want to earn money for your later education and don’t want a lot of student debt
  • You want job stability and a predictable, regular paycheck, regardless of where it takes you and your family
